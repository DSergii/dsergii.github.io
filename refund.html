Refund Policy

Last updated: 23 May 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with BuildEye AI. This Refund Policy describes when and how you can request a refund for a paid subscription.

All payments are processed by Paddle, our authorised reseller and Merchant of Record. Paddle handles all billing and refund transactions on our behalf.

1. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your subscription for any reason, you may request a full refund within 14 days of the initial purchase date. This applies to all paid plans (Start, Pro, Business) on their first payment.

To claim a refund under this guarantee:

  • Email us at drozdsa@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request".
  • Include the email address associated with your account and your Paddle order ID (found in your purchase receipt).

We will process your refund request within 5 business days. The refund will be returned to your original payment method by Paddle.

2. EU / EEA Right of Withdrawal

If you are a consumer residing in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the right to withdraw from a digital service contract within 14 days of purchase under the EU Consumer Rights Directive, without giving any reason.

Please note: if you have actively used the service (e.g., run AI analyses) during the withdrawal period, you may have waived this right under applicable law by expressly requesting the service before the withdrawal period expired. We will assess each case individually and aim to act fairly.

3. Renewal Refunds

Refunds are generally not available for subscription renewals. If you did not intend to renew, please make sure to cancel before the renewal date. Subscription cancellation can be done at any time from your account settings in the app.

As an exception, if a renewal charge occurs after you have already cancelled through the app (i.e., a technical error), we will refund the erroneous charge in full. Please report such cases to us within 7 days of the charge.

4. Free Plan

The Free plan incurs no charge, so no refunds apply.

5. Refunds for Downtime or Service Issues

If BuildEye AI experiences significant downtime or a confirmed technical failure that substantially prevents you from using paid features, you may contact us to request a proportional account credit or partial refund. We handle these cases on an individual basis.

6. How Long Refunds Take

Once we approve your refund, Paddle will process the transaction. Funds typically appear on your statement within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

7. Contact

To request a refund or ask about your billing, please email drozdsa@gmail.com with your account email and Paddle order ID. We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days.
